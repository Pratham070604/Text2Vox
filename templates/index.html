Frequently Asked Questions

What is Text2Vox?

Text2Vox is an AI-powered text-to-speech web application that converts written text into realistic speech in multiple voices and languages.

How does Text2Vox work?

You simply enter your text, choose a voice, and click "Generate." The app processes your text in real-time and provides a downloadable audio file.

What voice models are available?

Text2Vox supports multiple AI voices, each with unique tones and accents. More voices will be added in future updates.

Can I download the generated speech?

Yes! Once the audio is generated, you can download it in a supported format like MP3.

Does Text2Vox support multiple languages?

Yes, the app supports various languages for speech synthesis, allowing you to generate speech in different accents.

Can I convert PDFs or documents into speech?

Yes, Text2Vox allows you to upload documents (PDFs, text files, etc.) and convert their content into speech.

Is there a character limit for text input?

Currently, there may be a limit on text length, depending on system performance and voice synthesis capabilities.

Can I edit dialogues in Story Maker mode?

Yes! The Story Maker feature allows you to add multiple characters with individual dialogues and edit them before generating the full story.

Do I need an internet connection to use Text2Vox?

Yes, since the app uses real-time AI processing, an active internet connection is required.

Will more features be added in the future?

Yes! Future updates will include more voice models, better customization, enhanced playback options, and improved document support.
